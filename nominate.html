Registration & Nomination

Name someone the nation should thank

You cannot nominate yourself, and you cannot nominate the famous for being famous. You nominate a person whose work you have seen with your own eyes, over years — and you bring the evidence. This is how an unnoticed life becomes a pillar.

Faithful preview — nominations formally open in 2028. This intake works exactly as the real one will, but it stores and sends nothing. Do not enter another person's private details yet. Its purpose is to show the criteria and the shape of a nomination.

Who may be honoured, and who may nominate

An individual, living

One person, never an organization. Living, so their story and consent can be recorded.

Ten years of work

A sustained, verifiable contribution already made — not a promise, not potential.

You knew the work

You are a student, patient, colleague, neighbour, teacher, or the one they served — with at least two years and a hundred hours beside the work.

Seven witnesses

At least seven people who knew the work, from two kinds of relationship, at least one who was directly helped. Recorded oral testimony counts fully.

Seven evidences

At least seven independent evidence items, from two or more classes: records, independent testimony, or what a verifier can see on the ground.

No payment, no campaign

No one is ever paid to nominate, and public campaigning for a nominee disqualifies them. This is not a popularity contest.
